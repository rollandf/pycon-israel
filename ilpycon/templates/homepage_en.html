PyCon Israel 2018

PyCon Israel is happening on June 4-5, 2018! The event will be attended by hundreds of Pythonistas and sponsored by major international and local companies that use Python in their day-to-day business.

Join us for two days of learning from world-class keynotes and miryad presentations, all about the Python programming language, and don't forget to socialize, connect, and have fun!

PyData Track

Data Science and Engineering have become major fields in the last few years, in the industry in general, and the Python community in particular. PyCon Israel is proud to host a PyData track, for the second year in a row.

Django Girls

We are proud to cooperate with the Django Girls workshop, introducing women to programming for the web and beyond. A workshop will be held on June 3 at the Red Hat offices in Ra'anana. Registration is now open!

Open Source

Python itself, and most of its ecosystem, have always been Open Source software. PyCon Israel is organized under the auspices of Hamakor, the Israeli Open Source and Free Software Association.
